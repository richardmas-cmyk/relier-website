ABOUT RELIER

Founded by a team of entrepreneurs with decades of experience in Fintech, Remittance, Telecommunications and FMCG, RELIER was created to provide options to the millions of immigrants around the world helping their families and loved ones "back home". A technological platform of connectivity by API that allows to centralize, in a secure way, the connections of multiple providers of wallets and payment centers in different countries with multiple physical and digital distributors worldwide. Relier is a technology hub that allows you to easily unite multiple global financial services distributors with multiple wallets and local payment centers around the world. 1 connection, 1 contract, 1 single point of contact equals multiple distribution channels and thousands of physical and digital points of sale globally.

With Relier, we aim to empower all immigrants to aid their loved ones back home in a more efficient and cost effective way.
